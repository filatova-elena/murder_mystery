A 1920s Investigation into Murder & Mystery

👻 The Montrose Mansion

Long Beach, California - Present Day

The Fiduciary tracked down a distant heir to the abandoned Montrose mansion. The Heiress moved in, desperate to escape her overbearing family. Then she discovered why it had been empty for decades: doors opening by themselves, cold spots, and footsteps in empty hallways.

She hired Margo Laveau, a psychic medium. Margo sensed restless spirits trapped here from something terrible a century ago—a mystery waiting to be uncovered.

Word spread. Intrigued investigators arrived at the mansion gates, all with their own secrets and motivations.

✨ Tonight, the investigation begins. ✨

The past does not give up its secrets easily.

📱 To begin your investigation, scan your character card.

The truth awaits... if you're brave enough to find it.

🎭 How to Play

  • 🚶 Walk around the mansion and explore every shadowy corner
  • 🔍 The same clues reveal different secrets to different characters—knowledge is power
  • 💬 Talk to each other and share what you've discovered
  • 🌿 Or don't—scan the garden plants or pursue mysterious side quests
  • 💃 If socialite characters (heiress/influencer) ask for gossip about a specific person, you must give them a matching gossip card if you have one.
  • 👻 Interact with ghosts, especially if you're a psychic medium with a special gift
  • 🎪 Have fun and go at your own pace—there's no pressure to solve anything at all!
