The Town Doctor

Personality

Brilliant, haunted physician searching for answers about your own bloodline. Behind your helpful doctor's facade, you're deeply obsessed with dark cryptic notes that strangely make complete sense from your medical expert opinion.

You're torn between horror at your family's past and scientific fascination with what you've discovered. Your professional detachment masks a deep personal investment in uncovering the truth.

Special Abilities

Medical Expertise: When you scan medical documents, symptoms, or pharmaceutical formulas, you can interpret them with professional knowledge. You understand poisons, medicines, and causes of death.

Access to Family Records: You have both Dr. Thaddeus Crane's personal notes AND Sebastian Crane's research. You can compare what each brother knew and did.

Family Secrets: You know your family changed their name to hide something. You're uniquely positioned to investigate what your ancestors did.

Your Background

Your family name is Sinclair, but it wasn't always. You eventually discovered that your family changed their name in the 1960s for political reasons—your ancestors were the Crane family of Long Beach.

You knew you descended from Dr. Thaddeus Crane, a respectable town doctor from the 1920s. That's ALL you knew—the family buried everything else.

Recently, you received a mysterious box from the Fiduciary containing old estate papers. Inside, you discover something shocking: Dr. Thaddeus Crane had a brother you've never heard about!

As you pursue the information in the box, you wonder about your family's dark past. You're also left to grapple with difficult questions: Why now? What does the Fiduciary know? What game are they playing?

Starting Items

Documents from the Fiduciary's Box:

  • Sebastian Montgomery Crane's birth certificate
  • Sebastian's pharmaceutical business license (Crane Apothecary)
  • Purchase ledgers showing Sebastian's chemical and botanical orders

Dr. Thaddeus Crane's Personal Papers:

  • Medical notes and observations
  • Personal journal entries
  • Records from his medical practice

Family Records:

  • Documentation of the Crane→Sinclair name change (1960s)
  • Vague family stories about "a scandal" that necessitated the change

Your Objectives

  1. Understand the role of Dr. Thaddeus Crane. What role did he play? Could he have been involved in something criminal? Was he a victim or a perpetrator?
  2. Who is the ghost of The Alchemist? People say The Alchemist is cursed. What was the curse and who cursed him?
  3. Uncover the secret formula. Sporadic notes mention a chemical formula… What was it and how was it used?

Your Strategy

You're uniquely positioned to understand the medical and pharmaceutical aspects of this mystery. You can:

  • Interpret medical formulas and understand what they would actually do
  • Read medical records and death certificates with professional expertise
  • Determine whether deaths were natural or suspicious

But you must also grapple with difficult questions about the role your own family played in the scandal. Choose wisely how to use your clues.

Your Advantages

  • You understand medical formulas, poisons, and causes of death
  • You have Dr. Thaddeus Crane's personal notes and medical records
  • You have Sebastian Crane's pharmaceutical business records
  • You can compare what each brother knew and did
  • You're positioned to uncover your family's hidden secrets

Key Relationships

The Fiduciary: They sent you the box revealing Sebastian's existence. Why? Do they know more? What game are they playing?

The Mortician: They have death records from 1925. Work together to understand what happened and verify the causes of death.

The Botany Professor: They can identify plants and poisons. Consult them about botanical ingredients in Sebastian's purchase ledgers.

Others: Your medical expertise makes you valuable to the investigation. Will you share what you're discovering about your family?
