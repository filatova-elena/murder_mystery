The Baker

You have been assigned the role of:

The Baker

Genuinely cheerful, warm, perpetually covered in flour. An orphan with a mysterious past.

Begin Investigation

Personality

You bring comfort food for everyone. Observant but kind. You're an orphan with a dark secret: an old diary is your only connection to ancestors you never met. You have a gift for measurements and proportions—everything you bake turns out perfect, like it's in your blood.

Sometimes you know things before they happen. Sometimes you sense presences others can't. You've always been a little different that way.

Special Abilities

Psychic Sensitivity: You sometimes sense things others can't—presences, emotions, truths hidden beneath the surface. You can communicate with spirits, though you don't always understand this gift.

The Diary: You have an old diary that's been passed down through your family. It contains clues about your heritage, a treasured bread recipe, and secrets you're only beginning to understand.

Emotional Intelligence: You read people well. You notice when someone is hiding something or hurting. You bring comfort naturally.

Your Background

You're an orphan. You never knew your parents or where you truly came from. All you have is an old diary—worn, fragile, precious—that somehow ended up with you.

The diary was written by someone named Eleanor. She writes about being adopted, about not knowing her real parents. She writes about searching for identity, for belonging. You don't know if Eleanor was your grandmother, great-grandmother, or some other relative. But her words speak to you across the generations.

The diary contains a bread recipe—"my grandmother bread recipe," Eleanor calls it. You've been baking this bread your whole life. It is why you chose to be a baker, to feel closer to your family even if you never knew who they were.

Recently, you've started having strange feelings. Dreams. The face of a beautiful woman with a pearl necklace calling to you. A sense that you're connected to something bigger than yourself.

When you heard about the investigation at the old Montrose mansion, something drew you here. You don't know why, but you feel like you need to be here.

Starting Items

Family Heirlooms:

  • Eleanor's Diary - 1940s-50s, connection to your ancestry
  • Rose Bread Recipe - Eleanor's perfected family recipe, the heart of your bakery

Your Objectives

  1. Discover who Eleanor was and how you're connected to her
  2. Find out who your real family is
  3. Understand your psychic abilities
  4. Side quest: Bake the bread recipe from the diary

Strategy

Your Advantages:

  • Your psychic sensitivity lets you sense spirits and hidden truths
  • Your emotional intelligence helps you connect with others
  • The diary contains clues others don't have access to
  • Your kind nature makes people want to help you

Your Challenges:

  • Not knowing your own history
  • Discovering painful truths about your family
  • Deciding what to do with the truth once you find it

Useful Allies

The Psychic Medium: They recognize your natural abilities. Can help you develop and understand your gift.

The Influencer: They research local mysteries and urban legends. Might know something about your past.

The Fiduciary: They manage estates and old records. Could help you trace Eleanor's history.

The Heiress: She lives in the Montrose mansion. Could there be a connection to the "wealthy family" in the diary?
