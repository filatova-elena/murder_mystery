Main Mystery Clues

Investigation Clues Organized by Mystery Discovery

This document organizes all clues that lead to discovering the central mysteries of the case. Clues are grouped by what they reveal and the revelation they contribute to solving.

Mystery 1: What Really Happened to Alice Whitmore?

Official Story: Accidental fall. True Story: Murdered by Dr. Thaddeus Crane

Clues Revealing Alice Was Murdered (Not Accidental)

Death Certificate - Physical Evidence

Document
Base: "Death certificate for Alice Whitmore, Oct 12 1925, accidental fall"
Reveals: The injury patterns don't match a simple fall. There's evidence of blunt force trauma from a heavy object inconsistent with stairs.
Full Access: Doctor, Mortician (can see medical analysis)

Alice's Ghost Vision - Eyewitness Account

Vision
Reveals: Alice felt something heavy hit her head suddenly. She didn't see who it was. Blunt force. Then darkness. The truth that she was murdered, not an accident.
Full Access: Psychic Medium, Grandmother, Explorer

Silver Candle Holder - The Weapon

Artifact
Reveals: A heavy silver candle holder, now on a shelf. It matches the pattern of Alice's head injury. This is the murder weapon.
Full Access: Doctor (recognizes injury match), Mortician (finds injury inconsistencies)

Dr. Thaddeus Crane's Diary - The Confession

Journal
Reveals: Thaddeus's own handwriting: "Desperate and cornered, I acted. Alice was going to expose everything. I couldn't let that happen. On the stairs. A silver holder. I made it look like a fall."
Full Access: Doctor (sees the confession), Mortician (sees his complicity)

Alice's Ghost Partial Vision

Vision
Reveals: Fragmented emotional truth: "I told him to confess... I was going to tell... something hit me... darkness..."
Partial Access: Baker, Dressmaker (get vague emotional hints)

Mystery 2: What Really Happened to Cordelia Montrose?

Official Story: Natural causes (heart failure). True Story: Poisoned by corrupted elixir that Dr. Thaddeus created

Clues Revealing Cordelia Was Poisoned (Not Natural Death)

Death Certificate - Medical Evidence

Document
Base: "Cordelia Montrose, 1925, natural causes - heart failure"
Reveals: Cumulative cardiac glycoside toxicity. This is not natural causes—it's systematic poisoning with plant-based toxins over weeks, not instant.
Full Access: Doctor (sees the toxicology), Mortician (recognizes the pattern)

Cordelia's Ghost Vision - Full Account

Vision
Reveals: The complete story of slow poisoning—morning after morning drinking the tea with the elixir, gradual sickness, confusion, loving Sebastian while being betrayed, weakness, the realization something was terribly wrong.
Full Access: Psychic Medium, Grandmother, Baker, Dressmaker (they know her story completely)

Plant Specimens in Jars - The Poison Ingredient

Botanical
Reveals: Digitalis (foxglove), concentrated extract. This is what was ADDED to the elixir. A deadly cardiac poison.
Full Access: Botany Professor (identifies toxin), Fiduciary (recognizes as poison), Baker (intuitive sense of danger)

Dr. Thaddeus Crane's Diary/Notes - The Conspiracy

Journal
Reveals: "Cordelia threatened to expose me. I examined Sebastian's harmless elixir. I added the foxglove myself. Sebastian administers it unknowingly. She will not live to tell anyone."
Full Access: Doctor (sees full context), Mortician (sees his involvement)

Sebastian's Original Formula - The Key

Document / Botanical
Reveals: Sebastian's formula is HARMLESS—rose oil, damiana, valerian, honey, brandy. No poisons at all. He was making a genuine love elixir, not a weapon. Someone corrupted it.
Full Access: Botany Professor (botanical analysis), Doctor (pharmaceutical analysis)

Cordelia's Diary - The Threat She Made

Journal
Reveals: Cordelia confronted Dr. Thaddeus about his affair with Alice and threatened to expose him to the medical board if he didn't end it. This gave Thaddeus a clear motive to kill her.
Full Access: Heiress (family history), Fiduciary (sees the motive through records)

Mystery 3: What Was Sebastian Crane's Role?

Sebastian was an innocent victim. He unknowingly administered poison to Cordelia and died testing his own corrupted formula

Clues Revealing Sebastian Was Innocent (Not The Murderer)

Sebastian's Alchemical Formula/Notebook

Document
Base: "Alchemical formula with strange ingredients"
Reveals: Sebastian's original work is completely harmless—rose oil, damiana, valerian, honey. He was an idealistic romantic, not a poisoner. He believed he was making a love elixir.
Full Access: Botany Professor (identifies ingredients), Doctor (sees non-toxic formula)

Sebastian's Ghost Vision - His Perspective

Vision
Reveals: "My work is failing me. She's getting worse, not better. I don't understand. I tested the formula myself to see what was wrong. Then I was dying. What did I do wrong? What did I do wrong?"
Full Access: Psychic Medium, Grandmother (full visions)

Sebastian's Death Certificate - Poisoned by His Own Formula

Document / Medical
Reveals: "Sebastian Crane, October 12-13, 1925. Cause: Acute digitalis poisoning with laceration. He cut himself during preparation and ingested the poison."
Full Access: Doctor (full analysis), Mortician (recognizes the pattern)

The Pocket Watch - The Jupiter-Venus Conjunction Date

Artifact
Reveals: September 4, 1925, 6:14 AM. This is when Sebastian first gave the elixir to Cordelia during the astronomical conjunction. He was romantic and obsessed with cosmic timing. He had no idea he was poisoning her.
Full Access: Clockmaker (sees astronomical significance)

Timeline Evidence - The Slow Illness

Knowledge / Document
Reveals: September 4 to October 6: Sebastian gave daily doses to Cordelia. October 7: He tested the concentrated formula himself and became violently ill within hours. October 12-13: He died. This timeline shows he only discovered something was wrong AFTER weeks of unknowingly poisoning her.
Full Access: Doctor (sees medical timeline), Fiduciary (sees chronology)

Dr. Thaddeus's Diary - The Sabotage Confession

Journal
Reveals: "I examined Sebastian's harmless elixir. I added concentrated foxglove extract. He will unknowingly administer the poison daily. He will never suspect his own work is the weapon."
Full Access: Doctor (sees the full confession)

Mystery 4: Who Covered Up The Crimes?

Doctor Thaddeus Crane and Mortician Silas worked with the Montrose family to hide the truth

Clues Revealing the Cover-Up & Conspiracy

Payment Records - The Bribe

Document
Base: "Payment record from October 1925, $500 to mortician"
Reveals: Large payment from Montrose Family Trust with notation "Confidential arrangement." This is a bribe to keep silent about what really happened.
Full Access: Fiduciary (sees the conspiracy), Mortician (recognizes ancestor's payment)

Dr. Thaddeus Crane's Diary - His Guilt

Document / Medical
Reveals: Internal conflict, medical reasoning showing he KNEW these were poisonings, the decision to falsify causes of death, the guilt about the cover-up.
Full Access: Doctor (full context), Mortician (sees his complicity)

Pre-Nuptial Agreement - Witness Involvement

Document
Reveals: Dr. Thaddeus Crane is the witness to Sebastian and Cordelia's engagement. He was connected to Sebastian from the beginning. He knew about the relationship, the formula, possibly the plan.
Full Access: Fiduciary (legal analysis), Mortician (recognizes the connection)

False Death Certificates - Altered Records

Document
Reveals: The official causes of death don't match the medical evidence. Someone deliberately changed the narrative for all three deaths.
Full Access: Doctor (sees the falsehoods), Fiduciary (recognizes legal manipulation)

Montrose Family Trust Records

Document
Reveals: Shows payment for "discretion." The family made a conscious choice to cover up rather than expose scandal.
Full Access: Fiduciary (starting item), Heiress (family knowledge)

Mystery 5: Why? And What Were The Connections?

Understanding Sebastian's obsession and the web of relationships

Clues Revealing Motivations & The Bigger Picture

Knowledge: Family Background - Sebastian's Obsession

Knowledge
Reveals: Sebastian was obsessed with Cordelia, believed alchemy could make her love him, thought the Jupiter-Venus conjunction was cosmically significant. His delusion drove the murders.
Various characters know different pieces of this story

Knowledge: Cordelia's Baby - Eleanor

Knowledge
Reveals: Cordelia was pregnant by someone (Thomas?), gave birth, gave up the baby. This was her secret pain. Her engagement to Sebastian was meant to cover something up.
Full Access: Heiress (family history), Midwife/Baker (local knowledge)

Adoption Papers - Legal Loophole

Document
Reveals: Eleanor was never formally adopted by the Sullivan family. Legally, she remained Cordelia's heir. The Montrose inheritance passed to someone unexpected.
Full Access: Fiduciary (legal discovery)

Harbor Import & Trading Records - The Romano Connection

Document / Knowledge
Reveals: The bottles, the plants, possibly the poison ingredients came through the Romano smuggling operation. Was Romano involved? Or was Sebastian just using their supply chain?
Full Access: Art Collector (sees criminal connections), Explorer (knows shipping routes)

Alice's Connection - Witness or Threat?

Vision / Knowledge
Reveals: Alice discovered Sebastian's laboratory and his experiments. She threatened to expose him. He killed her to keep her silent.
Full Access: Psychic Medium/Grandmother (ghost vision), Explorer (Alice's vision)

Elias Monroe's Devotion - The Dressmaker's Story

Knowledge / Artifact
Reveals: Elias loved Cordelia genuinely. He made her wedding dress with perfect love. But she was betrothed to Sebastian, the man who would poison her. Elias may not have known, but he was part of the tragedy.
Full Access: Dressmaker (ancestor's memories and feelings)
