All Clues Reference

Complete Inventory - Locations, Possessions, and Access

This document lists every clue in the game with its location, who possesses/finds it, and which characters have access to it.

Journals & Diaries

Dr. Thaddeus Crane - Patient Notes

Type: Journal - Medical consultation notes from sessions with Alice
Possession: Town Doctor
Reveals: His sessions with Alice, the affair, emotional connection

Dr. Thaddeus Crane's Personal Journal

Type: Journal - Personal confessions and guilt
Possession: Town Doctor
Reveals: His various worries - guilt, anxiety, internal conflict about the events and cover-up

Sebastian's Alchemical Notes

Type: Journal - Formula notes and alchemical observations
Location: Torn and scattered, found in the laboratory
Reveals: Original HARMLESS formula - rose oil, damiana, valerian, honey. Shows Sebastian was innocent.

Elias Monroe's Poems

Type: Journal - Love poems for Cordelia
Possession: Dressmaker (Elias Monroe's descendant)
Reveals: Elias's genuine love for Cordelia, his heartbreak over her betrothal to Sebastian

Cordelia's Diary

Type: Journal - Personal thoughts and events
Possession: Heiress (found at the mansion)
Reveals: Rose bread recipe from Cordelia, mentions of the affair, threat to Thaddeus

Eleanor Sullivan's Diary

Type: Journal - Personal diary with recipe
Possession: Baker (Eleanor is the Baker's ancestor)
Reveals: Rose extract ingredient ("use sparingly") - specialized baker recipe from Cordelia. Connects Eleanor to her biological mother.

Official Documents

Death Certificates

Type: Official documents (3 total)
Victims: Alice (Oct 12, 1925), Sebastian (Oct 12-13, 1925), Cordelia (Oct 18, 1925)
Location: Fiduciary office
Access: Doctor (full medical analysis - sees the falsifications), Mortician (recognizes injury inconsistencies), Everyone (base information)
Reveals: Official causes vs. actual causes. Medical evidence of poisoning and blunt force trauma.

Autopsy Reports

Type: Medical documents (3 total)
Victims: Alice, Sebastian, Cordelia
Location: Mortuary
Access: Doctor (full analysis), Mortician (in their possession), Everyone (base information)
Reveals: Detailed medical findings - injury patterns, toxicology, cause of death details

Pre-Nuptial Agreement

Type: Legal document
Parties: Sebastian Crane & Cordelia Montrose
Location: Fiduciary office
Access: Fiduciary (full legal analysis), Doctor (sees Dr. Thaddeus as witness), Everyone (base information)
Reveals: Engagement date (August 15, 1925), Dr. Thaddeus as witness, property arrangements

Payment Records - $500 to Mortician

Type: Financial document
Possession: Mortician
Access: Fiduciary (sees the bribe clearly), Mortician (recognizes ancestor's payment), Everyone (base information)
Reveals: Montrose family paid mortician "for discretion" - clear cover-up payment

Adoption/Placement Papers

Type: Legal document
Child: Eleanor (Cordelia's daughter)
Location: Fiduciary office
Access: Fiduciary (full legal analysis - sees no formal adoption), Heiress (family knowledge), Everyone (base information)
Reveals: Eleanor was NEVER formally adopted - legally remained Cordelia's heir

Montrose Family Trust Records

Type: Financial/Legal document
Location: Fiduciary office
Access: Fiduciary (full - starting item), Heiress (family history), Everyone (base information)
Reveals: Payments for "discretion," family decisions to cover up scandal

Visions (Ghost QR Codes)

Alice's Ghost - 11 Visions

Type: Spectral manifestation
Location: Stairs/Laboratory (cycling encounters)
Access: Psychic Medium/Grandmother (full visions), Explorer (full Alice visions), Baker/Dressmaker (partial - vague hints), Clockmaker (static interference - timing)
Reveals: Alice felt something heavy hit her head. She didn't see who. Blunt force. Then darkness. She was murdered, not an accident.

Cordelia's Ghost - 11 Visions

Type: Spectral manifestation
Location: Bedroom/Piano (cycling encounters)
Access: Psychic Medium/Grandmother (full visions), Baker/Dressmaker (full Cordelia visions), Explorer (partial), Clockmaker (static interference - dating)
Reveals: Morning after morning drinking tea with the elixir. Gradual sickness. Loving Sebastian while being poisoned. Confusion. The realization something was wrong.

Sebastian's Ghost - 11 Visions

Type: Spectral manifestation
Location: Laboratory/Garage (cycling encounters)
Access: Psychic Medium/Grandmother (full visions), All others (partial - dark presence and guilt), Clockmaker (static interference - conjunction timing)
Reveals: "My work is failing me. She's getting worse. I tested the formula myself. Then I was dying. What did I do wrong?"

Rumors (Printed Cards)

Rumor Cards - Approximately 20 cards

Type: Hearsay - Only 2/3 are true
Possession: Distributed to all players or placed around venue
Collectors (Heiress/Influencer): Can ask others to share and must exchange when meeting each other
Everyone else: Must give up at least one card when interacting with collectors
Reveals: Mix of true, false, and partial-truth information about the case

Knowledge (Base Information)

Character-Specific Knowledge

Type: Background information each character knows
Examples:
- Heiress: Cordelia was a Montrose family member, her engagement was called off, she died in 1925
- Baker: Montrose family history with the town, catering details
- Doctor: Dr. Thaddeus Crane was a physician in town during that era
- Fiduciary: Legal history of the Montrose, Crane, and Whitmore families
- Explorer: Historical details about Long Beach, shipping routes, Romano family operations
- Art Collector: Provenance of objects, art world connections
- Dressmaker: Elias Monroe's love for Cordelia and the dress he made
